## 📖 Description
Welcome to the **tic-tac-toe** game! This is a minimal version of the classic Tic-Tac-Toe game built with Next.js. It helps you understand the Minimax algorithm while having fun. Whether you prefer playing against friends or testing your skills against AI, this game offers a simple yet enjoyable experience.

## 🕹️ How to Play
1. Open the downloaded file using your web browser.
2. Choose your opponent: play against another player or the AI.
3. Take turns placing your X or O on the grid.
4. The first player to align three symbols wins the game.
5. If all spaces are filled without a winner, it’s a tie.
